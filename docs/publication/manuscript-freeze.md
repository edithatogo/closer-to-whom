# Manuscript freeze gate

A manuscript analysis may be frozen only when:

- the national service census has a dated source and review freeze;
- clinical pathway and funding-status sources are frozen;
- public demand calibration is complete;
- every displayed scenario is classified as current, licensed/funding-uncertain, commissioning counterfactual, or exploratory infrastructure;
- assumptions, schemas, source digests, code revision, random seeds, solver versions, and result digests are archived;
- DSA, PSA, structural analysis, optimisation, MCDA, and VOI receipts pass;
- clinical, Māori health/equity, licence, ethics-scope, and claim-boundary reviews are recorded;
- synthetic fixture artefacts are excluded from the manuscript result directory.
