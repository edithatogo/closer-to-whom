# Travel, time, and cost estimation

## Travel

For each routing point and eligible site, retain road-network distance and time by time-of-day scenario. Where public timetable data support it, calculate public transport door-to-door time including access walk, waiting, transfers, and ferry components. Route engine, graph date, traffic assumption, query time, and cache fingerprint are part of the result provenance.

The offline great-circle/circuity engine exists only for software tests and early prototyping.

## Course burden

Travel is accumulated over the clinically meaningful course, including administrations and any reviews, investigations, cardiac monitoring, or concurrent-treatment visits affected by the configuration.

## Cost perspectives

- patient out-of-pocket: marginal vehicle running costs, parking, tolls, fares, accommodation, less reimbursement;
- patient and whānau: direct cost plus patient and companion time;
- payer: travel assistance and commissioned transport;
- provider: staff, vehicle, mobile-service, cold-chain, supplies, observation, and failed visits;
- societal: resource costs and time, excluding transfers such as reimbursement.

All prices retain a price year and analysis-date validity check. Gross travel burden, reimbursement, and net out-of-pocket burden are shown separately.
