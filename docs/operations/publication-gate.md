# Publication gate

The CTW-060 publication receipt separates release-artifact generation from
publication readiness. The repository may produce verification, manifest, and
readiness artefacts while the gate remains blocked. A reviewable or published
state requires completed national-analysis, source/licence, clinical, and
governance evidence receipts.

Artifact existence does not establish service capability, clinical eligibility,
observed capacity, authorisation, or permission to redistribute Healthpoint
data. The prohibited-content list is part of the machine-readable contract.
