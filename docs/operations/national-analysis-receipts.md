# National analysis receipts

`data/public/national-analysis-receipt.yaml` is the publication gate for the
CTW-050 scenario, optimisation, uncertainty, MCDA, and VOI outputs. The
repository contains reusable analysis primitives and synthetic demonstrations,
but the receipt remains `blocked_on_prerequisites` until the service, public
input, clinical, routing, and governance gates are complete.

Moving to a reviewable or completed state requires every prerequisite to be
marked complete and one receipt for each required output. This records evidence
readiness; it does not itself constitute clinical approval, Māori endorsement,
ethics/HDEC determination, observed capacity, or operational authorisation.
