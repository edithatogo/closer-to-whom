machine-readable GitHub-ecosystem catalogue and audit artefacts that were preserved
from a subsequent execution environment.

The ecosystem catalogue, pipeline definitions, tool contracts, generated report, and
audit JSON are useful implementation inputs. The files under `handover/inherited/`
are **reported evidence from that later environment**, not automatically reproduced
validation in this assembled tree. Re-run every gate locally and implement any
referenced ecosystem scripts, tests, schemas, or CI that are missing from the source
snapshot. Do not claim those reported checks as current until they are reproduced.

The original briefing is distributed at the outer archive level as reference material.
It is not part of the public-data pipeline and should not be committed to a public
repository without an explicit decision by its author.
